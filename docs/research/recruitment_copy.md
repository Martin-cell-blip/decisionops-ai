# Participant Recruitment Copy

## Short message

I am researching how finance and e-commerce operators handle reconciliation exceptions and SKU decisions. I am looking for people who completed one of these tasks in the last 30 days for a 25-minute, non-sales conversation. I will ask you to walk through a recent workflow; please do not share confidential records. Notes will be anonymized, and you may stop at any time.

## Screening questions

1. Which task did you personally complete in the last 30 days?
   - reconciled payments, invoices, carrier bills or marketplace payouts;
   - changed a SKU's price, inventory, assortment or campaign allocation.
2. Which tools or files were involved?
3. Did you try an automation, integration or BI tool? What happened?
4. Are you comfortable walking through the workflow without sharing customer or company-confidential data?

Only schedule participants who describe a specific recent action.

## Target sample

- 4-7 finance/bookkeeping participants;
- 3-7 merchant/operations participants;
- at least two people who abandoned an automation or reporting tool.
